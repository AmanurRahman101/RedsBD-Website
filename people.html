Our Team

REDS brings together experienced professionals in engineering, architecture, research, and operations — led by a director who values execution, integrity, and impact.

MT

M. Tanvir Alam

Director

M. Tanvir Alam brings over 14 years of executive leadership in business development, strategic planning, and large-scale project management. Holding an MBA from the Institute of Business Administration (IBA), University of Dhaka, and a B.Sc. in Computer Science from North South University, he specializes in building cross-functional teams, driving operational excellence, and leading major technical and institutional initiatives across various sectors.

SN

S. M. Nazmul Alam

Senior Civil Engineer & Operations Manager

With over a decade of hands-on experience in civil engineering and business development, S. M. Nazmul Alam holds a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from BUET. He has a proven track record in project management, contract documentation, procurement, and leading complex construction and infrastructure projects from inception to delivery.

MR

Ar. Mahbubul Hasan (Rana)

Principal Architect

Ar. Mahbubul Hasan is an accomplished architect registered with the Institute of Architects Bangladesh (IAB). Holding a Bachelor of Architecture and pursuing his Master of Architecture, he brings extensive experience in healthcare, commercial, high-rise residential, and interior design projects, delivering innovative structural and interior solutions.

AA

A. M. Ashraful Arefin

Senior Project Engineer

A. M. Ashraful Arefin is a Civil Engineering graduate from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology with specialized expertise in large-scale structural construction, project monitoring, and quality control. His portfolio includes industrial feed mill projects, high-rise residential developments, and commercial infrastructure.

SC

Sanjoy Chowdhury

Environment, Health & Safety (EHS) Lead Engineer

Sanjoy Chowdhury is a certified Lead Auditor and Chemical Engineer graduating from Shahjalal University of Science & Technology. He specializes in industrial safety, environmental impact mitigation, hazard analysis, and regulatory compliance, bringing extensive field experience from major corporate and industrial project sites.

Syed Nurul Islam

Electrical & Electronics Engineer
