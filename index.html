Introducing REDS

Research Evaluation and Development Studies (REDS) is built to function as a single worldwide partnership, bound together by a strong set of values, including a strong commitment to diversity, and a shared goal: to bring about long-lasting, positive change in the world. The goal of REDS is to create a great company that attracts, develops, inspires, and keeps extraordinary employees. REDS assists our clients in significantly improving their performance in a distinctive, long-lasting, and significant way.

In contemporary times, the pursuit of economic growth, sustainability, and inclusion has necessitated a departure from the belief that these goals are mutually exclusive. Today’s leaders are tasked with the imperative of achieving all three simultaneously. This emerging paradigm of growth is characterized by enhanced sustainability, fostering innovation while minimizing adverse environmental consequences, and fostering inclusivity by providing equitable access to opportunities for individuals across the globe.

Acknowledging the formidable nature of this endeavor, our unwavering aspiration is to assist organizations and their leaders in transforming this vision into tangible reality. On a daily basis, we forge partnerships with our clients worldwide, empowering them to establish audacious strategies, unleash the untapped potential of their workforce, integrate transformative technologies into their operations, and drive sustainable innovation. The consequential impact we facilitate enables our clients to attain lasting change in their capabilities and performance, propelling the transition towards sustainable and inclusive growth at an accelerated pace.

Our Mission

To assist our clients in achieving remarkable, enduring, and significant advancements in their performance, while simultaneously cultivating an outstanding organization that attracts, nurtures, strengthens, and retains exceptional individuals.

Our purpose as a research consultancy firm is to drive transformative insights and deliver actionable solutions that empower organizations to make informed decisions, innovate with confidence, and navigate the complex challenges of an ever-evolving landscape.

By leveraging our expertise, rigorous methodologies, and advanced analytics, we strive to facilitate our clients’ success by unlocking new opportunities, optimizing strategies, and accelerating their path towards sustainable growth and competitive advantage.

Who We Are

Our organization functions as an integrated and cohesive global partnership, driven by a shared set of principles that reinforce our collective identity. Central to our values is an unwavering dedication to promoting diversity, alongside a unified objective: to effect lasting and constructive transformation on a global scale.

As a life science research institute, REDS is committed to finding the basic discoveries necessary to preserve health and enhance wellbeing over the course of life. Our groundbreaking work in human biology ignites the creative breakthroughs that will enhance and safeguard lifetime health.

Our Commitment

We have a deep and longstanding commitment to advancing diversity, equity, and inclusion in business, in society, and within our firm. At REDS, diversity, equity, and inclusion are not just moral imperatives — they are integral to our dual mission: to help our clients make substantial, lasting performance improvements and to build a firm that attracts, develops, excites, and retains exceptional people.

Engineering & Development Services

Our Services

REDS provides a comprehensive range of engineering, planning, and socio-economic services for infrastructure and development projects from concept to completion.

Highways & Motorways

Feasibility studies, engineering design, construction supervision, and maintenance management for expressways, national highways, and urban road networks.

Bridges & Structures

Design and construction support for bridges, overpasses, culverts, and major structural works, including structural analysis and seismic considerations.

Railways

Project development from feasibility to detailed design and procurement, covering track alignment, station planning, and rail systems integration.

Planning & Architecture

Master plans for townships, industrial zones, and university complexes, plus architectural design for institutional, commercial, and residential buildings.

Energy

Energy concepts and implementation for increasing demand, including power generation, renewable energy planning, and energy efficiency studies.

Urban Transportation

Urban mobility planning to reduce journey times, ease congestion, and promote sustainable transport systems including BRT and mass transit.

Socio-Economic Management

Feasibility studies, financial requirements, economic impact assessments, and social impact evaluations for development and infrastructure projects.

Environment

Balancing growth with resiliency and environmental compliance, including environmental impact assessments, mitigation planning, and climate adaptation.

Water Resource Engineering

Flood flow discharge studies, river basin management, irrigation and drainage design, bank protection, and water conservation projects.

Ports

Port planning, berth design, cargo handling studies, and marine infrastructure development for coastal and inland waterway terminals.

Rural Development

Rural healthcare, education, infrastructure, and poverty reduction programs, including community-driven development and agricultural support.

Information & Communication Technology

Creating effective technical teams and digital solutions for commercial and engineering projects, including IT infrastructure and project management systems.

Tropical Medicine & Infectious Disease

Specialized clinical research, diagnostic studies, and epidemiological surveillance for malaria, dengue, tuberculosis, and emerging infectious diseases.

Non-Communicable Disease

Focused research, policy review, and prevention programs for chronic cardiovascular conditions, diabetes, oncology, and metabolic health disorders.

Maternal Child-Health

Supporting neonatal development, infant survival strategies, maternal healthcare improvements, and clinical interventions for children.
