Welcome To Research Evaluation and Development Studies

REDS is a research and development consultancy dedicated to rigorous evaluation, evidence-based policy advice, and people-centred development studies. We partner with government agencies, development partners, and civil society to design studies, measure impact, and turn findings into practical action across Bangladesh and the wider region.

Impact Evaluation

Design and delivery of rigorous impact evaluations using experimental and quasi-experimental methods to measure what works, for whom, and why.

Survey Research

Large-scale household, facility, and enterprise surveys with robust sampling, instrument design, enumerator training, and quality-assured data management.

Policy Analysis

Evidence synthesis and policy analysis that help decision-makers weigh options, anticipate trade-offs, and craft actionable recommendations.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Results frameworks, indicator systems, and adaptive M&E that keep programmes accountable and responsive throughout the project cycle.

Capacity Building

Tailored training and mentoring for institutions and field teams on research methods, evaluation practice, and data use for decision-making.

Social Development

Studies on livelihoods, social protection, inclusion, and community development that centre the experiences of the people programmes aim to serve.

Economic Research

Applied economic analysis covering markets, labour, poverty dynamics, and the fiscal and distributional effects of public programmes.

Gender & Inclusion

Gender-responsive research and equity-focused evaluations that surface barriers, opportunities, and pathways to more inclusive outcomes.

Climate & Environment

Research on climate resilience, natural resource management, and environmental safeguards linked to development investments.

Health Systems Research

Facility assessments, service-delivery studies, and evaluations that strengthen health programmes and improve access to quality care.

Education Research

Learning assessments, education sector studies, and programme evaluations that support better teaching, access, and student outcomes.

Data Analytics & ICT

Digital data collection, dashboards, and analytics that turn complex datasets into clear insights for programme managers and policymakers.
