Welcome To Research Evaluation & Development Studies

REDS functions as an integrated and cohesive partnership built to deliver transformative insights and actionable solutions. Bound together by a strong set of values and a shared goal to bring about long-lasting, positive change, we assist organizations and their leaders in transforming their vision into tangible reality. Expertise Areas

Our Core Divisions

Tropical Medicine & Infectious Disease

Specialized clinical research, diagnostic studies, and epidemiological surveillance for malaria, dengue, tuberculosis, and emerging infectious diseases.

Non-Communicable Disease

Focused research, policy review, and prevention programs for chronic cardiovascular conditions, diabetes, oncology, and metabolic health disorders.

Maternal Child-Health

Supporting neonatal development, infant survival strategies, maternal healthcare improvements, and clinical interventions for children.

Nutrition, Vaccine & Preventive Health

Conducting large-scale immunization trials, community nutritional assessments, and preventative healthcare program evaluations.

Health Service & Policy

Analytical research into healthcare system design, healthcare financing, delivery models, and government policy frameworks.

Global Health

Coordinating cross-border partnerships, international clinical standards, global disease control initiatives, and health equity.

Laboratory Science & Transfusion

Leading hematology research, advanced blood bank protocols, laboratory quality control, and clinical transfusion safety.

Training, Education & Communication

Developing capacity-building workshops, medical training programs, and evidence-based public health communication campaigns.

Medico-Legal & Human Rights

Aligning medical practice standards with human rights, medical law compliance, ethics reviews, and forensic advocacy.

Genetics & Biotechnology

Exploring advanced genomics research, genetic diagnostics, biotech vaccine development, and precision medicine solutions.

Biomedical Health Science

Integrating engineering technology with health sciences, advanced biomedical equipment, and digital health applications.

Social & Mental Health Development

Promoting community-focused psychiatric studies, stress counseling protocols, and cognitive health support systems.
