Our Core Services

REDS provides end-to-end research and evaluation support—from scoping and design through fieldwork, analysis, and dissemination. Whether you need a nationally representative survey, a mixed-methods impact evaluation, or advisory support on results frameworks, we assemble the right team and deliver clear, decision-ready evidence.

Impact Evaluation

Rigorous experimental and quasi-experimental evaluations that isolate programme effects and explain mechanisms of change for funders and implementers.

Survey Research

Sampling design, CAPI/CATI fieldwork, quality control, and clean datasets for household, facility, and enterprise studies of any scale.

Policy Analysis

Structured reviews and policy briefs that connect evidence to options, costs, and implementation realities for government and development partners.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Theory of change, indicator frameworks, baseline/midline/endline studies, and learning systems that keep programmes adaptive and accountable.

Capacity Building

Custom training and coaching for research units, M&E teams, and field supervisors on methods, ethics, and data use.

Social Development

Livelihoods, social protection, and community development studies with strong qualitative and participatory components.

Economic Research

Applied microeconomics, poverty analysis, and cost-effectiveness work that informs programme design and public investment choices.

Gender & Inclusion

Gender analysis, GESI-integrated evaluations, and research that elevates the voices of women, youth, and marginalised groups.

Climate & Environment

Resilience assessments, environmental and social studies, and evaluations of climate-adaptive and green development programmes.

Health Systems Research

Service readiness assessments, utilisation studies, and programme evaluations that support stronger health systems and outcomes.

Education Research

Learning assessments, school and teacher studies, and evaluations of education reforms and skills programmes.

Data Analytics & ICT

Digital data collection platforms, visualisation dashboards, and analytics that make complex evidence accessible to non-specialists.
