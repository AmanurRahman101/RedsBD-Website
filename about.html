About Us

Introducing REDS

Research Evaluation and Development Studies (REDS) is an independent consultancy focused on high-quality research, rigorous evaluation, and practical development studies. We support governments, development partners, NGOs, and private clients who need credible evidence to design, improve, and account for programmes that improve people’s lives.

Our teams combine field experience across Bangladesh with strong methodological grounding in survey research, impact evaluation, qualitative inquiry, and policy analysis. We work from study design through data collection, analysis, and clear communication of findings to decision-makers.

Our Mission

  • Helping clients turn evidence into better decisions is our mission
  • Upholding integrity, transparency, and methodological rigour in every study
  • Centring communities and programme participants in how we learn and evaluate
  • Building local capacity so research and M&E skills endure beyond each assignment

Corporate History

REDS was established to fill a growing need for independent, locally grounded research and evaluation capacity. From early assignments in social development and education, the firm has expanded into health systems, climate resilience, gender and inclusion, and digital data systems—always with a focus on usable evidence rather than reports that sit on a shelf.

Today REDS operates as a multidisciplinary practice, drawing on statisticians, economists, social researchers, and sector specialists who collaborate across assignments and mentor emerging researchers.

Research & Methods Unit

Our Research & Methods Unit maintains standards for sampling, instrument design, ethics, data security, and analysis. The unit supports quality assurance on every assignment and invests in continuous improvement of field protocols and analytical toolkits.

Learning & Capacity Centre

Through short courses, on-the-job mentoring, and partner workshops, REDS strengthens institutional ability to commission, manage, and use evaluation. We believe durable impact comes when clients can ask better questions—and interpret answers with confidence.
